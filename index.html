Mathematically me, very briefly

Starting in October 2025, I will be a Ph.D. student in Mathematics at Université Paris-Saclay, Paris (France), under the supervision of Prof. Luca Nenna and Prof. Simona Rota-Nodari.

The macro-set that encompasses most of my research interests is deeply rooted in the Calculus of Variations and the study of variational methods for solving minimum problems.

My Ph.D. research project will deal with regularized optimal transport problems.
