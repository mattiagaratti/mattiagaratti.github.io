My goal is to make learning an effective and enjoyable experience!

Private Lessons for University and Secondary School Students

I offer private lessons tailored to students' needs, covering different ambits.

  • University exam preparation: personalized support in Mathematics to help students be effectively prepared for exams, clarifing complex topics and strengthening problem-solving skills.
  • Secondary school tutoring: dedicated lessons for the second-grade secondary school students, focusing on building a solid foundation in Mathematics and Physics through clear explanations and practical exercises.
  • Remedial exam assistance: specialized help for second-grade secondary school students with suspended judgment in Mathematics and Physics, with targeted reviews and practice tests to ensure success.
  • Introduction to LaTeX: a beginner-friendly guide to the LaTeX typesetting language, ideal for students who want to create professional-quality academic documents or theses.

Lessons are flexible, adapting to each student's pace and learning style. For more details or to schedule a session, feel free to contact me!
